  • Basics

  • Will the crop be fresh and healthy?

    Yes, the crops will be fresh and healthy because it directly come from farmers field and it will be delivered to your home.

  • How do we believe the crops are healthy and fresh?

    You can trust the crops which we deliver you if in-case you have doubt regarding the freshness of the crops we will send the picture of the crop along with farmer's field photo and for a proof we can provide soil test report which is done by farmers.

  • As a farmer can i expect a reasonable profit after selling the crops?

    yes you can expect the profit because you can sell the crops with the reasonable profit with direct interaction of customers.

  • Can i except the crops to my door steps?

    yes you can except the crops to your door steps because we are responsible for shipment.

  • Payments

  • Do the website provide online payment gateway?

    No, the website will not provide online payment gateway. if you want to make a payment, the farmers and customers details will be provided in the website you can contact through the phone and get account details and make a payment through online mode which ever you are comfortable with for example: net banking.

  • If the payment gateway is not provided then how about the shipping payment to be paid?

    Shipping payment can be done in the same way like for example:net banking/cash the transport company details will be provided along with source to destination point you can go through the details provided by transport company and per km the amount will be calculated and charged according along with petrol/diesel fuel used.

  • How do we order crops or groceries online without payment gateway?

    we are giving a option for customer to order crops and groceries online you no need to worry about the order is placed or not that will be taken care by us but the payment gateway is between farmers and customers so it should be discussed between among yourself.

  • Delivery

  • What should I do if my order hasn't been delivered yet?

    if your have not received the delivery yet you can contact to the help line number which is there in the website so that we will resolve your issue.

  • Who takes care of shipping?

    The shipment will be taken care by the transport company.

  • When will my order arrive?

    after the order is placed the crops will be delivered within 2-3 days and sometimes it may depend on km/distance.
